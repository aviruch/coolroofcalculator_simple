What We Do

In India there is an increase in trend of high end homes and resort to have steep sloped roofs particularly in places with high precipitation and coastal regions. These high end homes are typically air conditioned and have a high potential for energy savings.

Using cool roofing solution could help in bringing down the air conditioning energy consumption. Based on the market input collected by the collaborator SGRI, shingles are preferred to be used in sloped roofs for better moisture management, thermal benefits and aesthetics. Saint Gobain marketing has also given an input that the most common slope used in the installation is 30o.
